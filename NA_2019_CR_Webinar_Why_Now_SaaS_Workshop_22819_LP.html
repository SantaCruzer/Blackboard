About the webinar

Over 500 Learn institutions have made the move to SaaS deployment, what can SaaS do for you?

Join Blackboard’s Jason Hardin and Francesca Monaco as they discuss how deploying SaaS at your institution can eliminate upgrade downtime and give you greater scalability, reliability, innovation and peace of mind. Follow the discussion and get your questions answered directly from our product experts. You’ll discover the ins and outs of SaaS and what it can do for you.

During the webinar, you’ll learn:
  • Why IT and Academic Technology leaders are embracing the cloud
  • How your institution can benefit from moving to SaaS
  • Where to start when planning a move to SaaS

Who is this webinar for?

  • Leaders in Academic Technology, Digital Learning, Distance Learning, Online Learning
  • Blackboard system administrators

Speakers

Jason Hardin

Senior Product Manager,
Blackboard

Jason has been focused on the project management of learning management systems for the last 11 years. He originally started with Blackboard through the Moodlerooms acquisition and focused on all aspects of hosting, maintaining and enhancing Blackboard’s SaaS Moodle LMS. Last year Jason joined the Learn product management team and uses his expertise to help Blackboard define and manage security, data privacy and hosting for Learn Managed Hosting and SaaS.

Francesca Monaco

LMS Marketing Director,
Blackboard

Francesca joined Blackboard 8 years ago as a member of the Learn product team. Back in 2011, she focused on the Community Engagement and Content Management capabilities of Learn and her role has expanded over the years to support the full Learn product. Francesca has become one of the faces of Learn, as you may recognize her from roadmap webinars, cohort programs and events like BbWorld.
